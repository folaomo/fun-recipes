Noodles, vegetables and egg



Description

Indomie is produced by Indofood, the pioneer of instant noodles in Indonesia
and is one of the largest instant noodles manufacturers in the world.
Indomie comes in many varieties from the classic soup flavours such as
Chicken, Vegetable, and Chicken Curry, to our most popular flavour Indomie Mi Goreng

This indomie is spiced with a few vegetable combinations as well as hard boild egg,
this is to introduce some greens and proteins to the mostly carborhydrate dish.
Most people and especially Nigerians regards noddles as a child's food
and would have nothing to do with it as a meal but following this special recipe,
you will get a diffeent perspective on indomie noodles and a longing to finally try out the dish.

Ingredients


  • 2 hard-boiled Eggs
  • 1 Cup Diced Carrots
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Green bean
  • 2 pack Noodles
  • Noodle Follow-come spices

Steps

  1. Dice the carrots and chop the green bean
  2. Add two cups of water to a pot on the stove and allow to boil
  3. Add the two pack of noodles to boiling water an allow to boil for three minutes
  4. Add chopped vegetables and the follow come spices
  5. Mix together to comine and steam on low heat for another two minutes
  6. serve with hard-boiled eggs
